ingredients:
  dough:
    - 1 to 1 ½ cup sourdough starter, bubbly and active (½ cup)
    - 1 ½ cup water (1 cup)
    - 3 tablespoons honey (1 tbsp)
    - 3 teaspoons salt (2 tsp)
    - 6 cups unbleached all purpose flour (4 c)
  boiling water:
    - 2 quarts water
    - 1 tablespoon baking soda
    - 1 tablespoon brown suga
directions:
- Mix **sourdough starter**, **water**, **honey**, **salt**, and **2 c. flour** in a mixer with a dough hook. Mix several minutes on low speed; to make a stiff dough.
- Add remaining **2 c. flour** half a cup at a time and continue kneading with dough hook until dough is smooth and pliable.
- Cover and allow to rise and ferment for 8-12 hours, or overnight.
- After fermentation, divide into 12 (8) equal pieces and shape into bagels.
- Cover with a light towel and allow to rise 1-4 hours.
- Preheat oven to **425 degrees**.
- Boil the **water** and add the **baking soda** and **sugar**.
- Using a slotted spoon, add bagels to the water and boil for one minute on each side, then put on a baking sheet.
- Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden.
