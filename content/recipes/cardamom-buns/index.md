ingredients:
  dough ingredients:
  - 4 cups white bread flour
  - ½ cup caster sugar (superfine sugar)
  - 1 teaspoon fine salt
  - 4 teaspoons instant dried yeast
  - 1 ½ cup milk, warmed to 37°C/98°F
  - 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  filling ingredients:
  - 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened (8 tbsp)
  - 1/4 cup granulated sugar (½ c)
  - 1-2 teaspoons ground cardamom (2-4 tsp)
  - 1/4 teaspoon fine salt
  topping:
  - 1 egg, lightly beaten with 1 tsp water
  - 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  - 2-4 teaspoons ground cardamom
  
directions:
- Mix **flour**, **sugar**, **salt** and **yeast** together with a dough hook.
- Gradually add **warm milk**, mixing until it forms a shaggy dough.
- Add the **butter**, one tablespoon at a time, mixing fully before adding another spoonful.
- After all butter has bee added, knead on medium speed for 15 to 20 minutes. The dough should be soft and smooth, pulling away from the sides of the bowl, but still somewhat sticky.
- Let the dough rise for **1 to 1 ½ hours**.
- Near the end of the rising time, mix together **cardamom sugar filling**.
- Roll the dough out into a rectangle approximately 20 x 16 inches.
- Spread the **cardamom sugar** filling over one side of the dough, then fold in half long ways.
- Cut the dough into half-inch strips to make about 16 strips.
- Shape each strip into a knot by winding the dough around fingers, and then crossing the last bit over the top and under.
- Put the shaped buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and let rise for 30 minutes.
- Prehead the oven to **390 F**.
- Brush the buns with **egg wash** and sprinkle with **cardamom sugar** just before baking.
- Bake for about 20 minutes, until lightly browned; check after 10 minutes and cover with foil if needed.
